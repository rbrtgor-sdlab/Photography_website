Biography

Hi, my name is Robert Gor Onyango, and I am a passionate photographer with over 10 years of experience in capturing life's most precious moments. My journey into photography began when I was a child, and I have been honing my skills ever since. I specialize in various genres of photography, including wedding, nature, birthday, and graduation photography. My work is characterized by its vibrant colors, attention to detail, and ability to tell a story through images. I believe that every photograph has the power to evoke emotions and create lasting memories. Through my portfolio, I aim to share my love for photography and inspire others to see the world through a different lens.
